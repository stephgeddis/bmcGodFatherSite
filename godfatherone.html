Synopsis

This gangster crime drama, based on The Godfather Novel by Mario Puzo ,is centered around a notorious Italian-American crime family known as the Corleone Family which is leaded by Don Vito Corleone (Marlon Brando) A.K.A The Godfather. When the Godfather's youngest son, Michael Corleone (Al Pacino) hastely joins the Mafia, he becomes involved greatly the criminal life-style from violence to betrayal.The more Michael attempts to maintain a normal life with his wife, Kay Corelone(Diane Keaton), the further he is drawn into the family business and the chaos and madness it brings.

Release Date: 24th August 1972
Director: Francis Ford Coppola
Runtime: 2 hours 58 minutes
