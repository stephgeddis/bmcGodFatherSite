Synopsis

In this sequel to the critically accalimed GodFather we get insight to earlier life of Don Vito Corelone (Robert De Niro) and get to see how Michael (Al Pacino) his son has been getting on with becoming the new power head of the Corleone crime family. In this sequel we see Michael being forced to deal with many rising issues whilst a young immigrant Vito Corelone is slowly introduced to the criminal life-style.

Release Date: 12th Decemember 1974
Director: Francis Ford Coppola
Runtime: 3 hours 20 minutes
